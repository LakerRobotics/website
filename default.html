Meet the Lakers

Team 5053, Laker Robotics, is a FIRST Robotics Competition team located at Our Lady of the Lakes Catholic School in Waterford, Michigan. Laker Robotics was formed in 2014, with the assistance of our friends on Team 33, The Killer Bees. Our team currently consists of 10 dedicated students, and while on the team, these students are being inspired to pursue their future careers, usually in the science and technology fields.
